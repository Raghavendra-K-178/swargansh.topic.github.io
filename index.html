"SWARGANSH" - A GLIMPSE OF HEAVEN

Our designed city, Swargansh, reimagines Delhi (28.7¬∞ N, 77.1¬∞ E) with innovative concepts inspired by the Design and Analysis of Algorithms course. Spanning an area of 1,500 sq. km, the city is meticulously divided into functional zones:

  • Residential Zone: 35%
  • Commercial Zone: 10%
  • Industrial Zone: 10%
  • Public Spaces: 15%
  • Agricultural Zone: 10%
  • Transport Infrastructure: 10%
  • Tourist Spots: 10%

Designed to be self-sustainable, Swargansh accommodates a population of 5,580,000, with the capacity to host an additional 400,000 migrants and tourists. Its revenue models and sustainability initiatives ensure long-term viability and resilience, making it a modern utopia that balances growth with ecological harmony.
