Governmental and Hospitality Case Studies

1. Governmental Case Study

Problem 1: Inefficient Resource Allocation for Public Services

SDG Goal: Goal 16 (Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions)

Indicator: 16.6.1 (Primary government expenditures as a proportion of the original approved budget)

Solution: Use Kruskal's Algorithm to build a minimum spanning tree, optimizing resource allocation with minimal cost.

Problem 2: Delays in Citizen Grievance Redressal

SDG Goal: Goal 16 (Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions)

Indicator: 16.7.1 (Proportions of population satisfied with public service decision-making)

Solution: Use Breadth-First Search (BFS) to prioritize grievance resolution by finding the shortest path to the appropriate department.

2. Hospitality and Service Case Study

Problem 1: Suboptimal Room Assignment in Hotels

SDG Goal: Goal 8 (Decent Work and Economic Growth)

Indicator: 8.9.1 (Tourism direct GDP as a proportion of total GDP)

Solution: Use Prim's Algorithm to allocate rooms optimally based on guest preferences and room availability.

Problem 2: Delay in Order Fulfillment in Restaurants

SDG Goal: Goal 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production)

Indicator: 12.3.1 (Global food loss index)

Solution: Use Floyd-Warshall Algorithm to optimize kitchen workflows by computing the shortest processing times between kitchen stations.
