Transportation and Healthcare Case Studies

1. Transportation Case Study

Problem 1: Traffic Congestion in Urban Areas

SDG Goal: Goal 11 (Sustainable Cities and Communities)

Indicator: 11.2.1 (Proportion of population with convenient access to public transport)

Solution: Use Dijkstra's Algorithm to calculate shortest paths, reducing congestion and improving navigation.

Problem 2: Inefficient Public Transport Scheduling

SDG Goal: Goal 9 (Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure)

Indicator: 9.1.2 (Passenger and freight volumes by mode of transport)

Solution: Use Breadth-First Search (BFS) to optimize public transport schedules and balance passenger flow.

2. Healthcare Case Study

Problem 1: Delays in Emergency Medical Response

SDG Goal: Goal 3 (Good Health and Well-being)

Indicator: 3.8.2 (Proportion of population with access to emergency services)

Solution: Use Floyd-Warshall Algorithm to precompute all-pairs shortest paths for efficient emergency response routing.

Problem 2: Resource Allocation in Hospitals

SDG Goal: Goal 3 (Good Health and Well-being)

Indicator: 3.c.1 (Health worker density and distribution)

Solution: Use Prim's Algorithm to design a minimum spanning tree for efficient resource allocation across hospitals.
